How to Prevent?

Wear a Mask

Wearing a properly-fitting mask, as well as vaccination, self-testing, and physical distancing, can help protect you and others by lowering the risk of COVID-19 spreading.

Wash your Hand

Hands should be washed often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. This is especially important after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing and before eating or preparing food. or If soap and water aren't available, use hand sanitizer. Cover all surfaces of your hands with an alcohol-based hand sanitizer containing at least 60% alcohol and rub them together until they feel dry.

Consult a Doctor

Get it checked out by a doctor even if you only have minor respiratory symptoms.

Clean Everyday

Use disinfectants and cleansers throughout the house. Clean apparent unclean surfaces with soap or detergent-based home cleansers. After that, cleanse the area using a home disinfectant.
